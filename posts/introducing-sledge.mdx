## What is Sledge?

Sledge is a multipurpose app that empowers Shopify and Shopify Plus merchants to boost their development time and grow their brands. Sledge provides important features that merchants can use in just a few minutes while merchants can focus on building other store features.

## Why Sledge?

Simplicity and Flexibility: Sledge is built with simplicity in mind. Its minimalistic approach doesn't compromise on flexibility, making it a great choice for both beginners and seasoned developers.

<br />
Performance-Oriented: Sledge's architecture is geared towards optimizing performance.
Your web applications will load faster and run smoother, delighting users and improving
search engine rankings.
<br />
Community-Driven: Sledge benefits from a vibrant and growing community. This means
regular updates, a wealth of resources, and a network of fellow developers ready
to assist.
<br />
Seamless Integration: Whether you're working on a new project or adding Sledge to
an existing one, its seamless integration makes the transition hassle-free.
